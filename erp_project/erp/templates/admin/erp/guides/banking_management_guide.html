account_balance Example Company: "Fashion Tex Ltd"

arrow_downward Step 2: Incoming Payment (টাকা আসা)

💰 Customer Payment Receive

Scenario: ABC Garments ৳3,57,000 টাকা bank transfer করেছে invoice INV-2024-001 এর জন্য।

  • Amount: ৳3,57,000
  • Bank Account: DBBL - Main
  • Payment Method: Bank Transfer
  • Reference: TXN-123456
  • Invoice: INV-2024-001

💵 Other Income (Bank Interest)

Scenario: Bank থেকে ৳5,000 interest পাওয়া গেছে।
  • Amount: ৳5,000
  • Bank Account: DBBL - Main
  • Income Type: Bank Interest
  • Entry: Debit Bank, Credit Interest Income

arrow_upward Step 3: Outgoing Payment (টাকা যাওয়া)

💸 Supplier Payment

Scenario: XYZ Fabrics কে ৳2,50,000 টাকা payment করতে হবে purchase invoice এর জন্য।
Banking → Outgoing Payments → Add

Payment Details

  • Supplier: XYZ Fabrics Ltd
  • Amount: ৳2,50,000
  • Bank Account: DBBL - Main
  • Payment Method: Cheque
  • Cheque No: 123456
  • Invoice: PINV-2024-001

📊 Accounting Entry

Debit: Accounts Payable ৳2,50,000
Credit: DBBL Bank Account ৳2,50,000

👥 Salary Payment

Scenario: মাসিক salary ৳8,00,000 টাকা City Bank থেকে payment করতে হবে।
  • Amount: ৳8,00,000
  • Bank Account: City Bank - Salary
  • Payment Type: Bulk Transfer
  • Entry: Debit Salary Expense, Credit Bank

check_circle Step 4: Bank Reconciliation (ব্যাংক মিলকরণ)

🔍 Monthly Reconciliation

Purpose: Bank statement এর সাথে আমাদের records match করা।
Banking → Bank Reconciliation → Add

📋 Reconciliation Process

  1. Bank statement download করুন
  2. System এর bank balance check করুন
  3. Outstanding cheques identify করুন
  4. Bank charges/interest add করুন
  5. Differences resolve করুন

📊 Example Reconciliation

Item Amount
Book Balance ৳12,50,000
Outstanding Cheques (-) ৳50,000
Bank Charges (-) ৳2,000
Bank Statement Balance ৳11,98,000

tips_and_updates Best Practices (সেরা পদ্ধতি)

✅ Daily Recording: প্রতিদিন সব bank transactions record করুন
✅ Proper Documentation: সব payments এর জন্য voucher এবং supporting documents রাখুন
✅ Monthly Reconciliation: প্রতি মাসে bank reconciliation করুন
✅ Segregation of Duties: Payment approval এবং recording আলাদা person করুন
✅ Regular Backup: Bank data এর regular backup রাখুন
